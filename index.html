ABOUT US

The Global Group was incorporated in April of the Year 1999 with a vision to become an integrated service provider, addressing the growing complexities of worldwide logistics systems.

To fulfill this dream, we first established ourselves as a complete logistics provider by partnering with organizations worldwide that share our objectives.

Shipping Agency representation was the next logical step the Group took, which further complemented the Logistics Arm.

Today the companies are well established in their areas of expertise, and are providing Complete Logistics Solutions to the trade.

Success of any organization is solely dependent on its people, and we are no exception. The strength of the company is its Management with over 30 years of experience in the Industry; having spearheaded carriers like Choyang Lines & Mediterranean Shipping Company in their last assignments. They are further complemented with a team of well-experienced professionals who are young, energetic, and are fully dedicated to satisfy the requisitions of its clientele in every field of transportation.

The Group continues to invest in human resources and information technology to keep abreast with the ever-changing needs, and requirements of the transportation industry, thus ensuring that both its clientele, and principals get the best of services.

VISION

To position PT. Global Freight Semesta as a truly Global Enterprise with Group offices worldwide & providing Total Logistics Solutions integrated with the best of Information Technology being practiced.

MISSION

To be a “One Stop Logistics Enterprise” serving as a “Strategic Link” in our clients' supply chain activities, thus creating, and enhancing value for our clients, employees, shareholders, and partners on a worldwide scale.

Further, to continuously strive for improvisation on our human resource capital to achieve quality customer and employee satisfaction.

Logistics

In a short span of time, “Global” has built up a network of agencies throughout the world. Special emphasis was given to seek partners who had the same vision and expertise thus complementing each other.

“Global”; A single source of all transportation needs has the abilities to offer its growing clientele specialized services such as:

  • Warehousing and Distribution
  • Local Haulages (Pick-up and Delivery)
  • International Removals
  • Custom Clearances
  • Door to Door Shipments
  • Documentation Preparation
  • FCL / LCL Consolidations
  • Ocean Freight for Imports and Exports
  • Air Freight for Import and Export
  • Sea / Air Combine Cargo

Logistics

With the 'JUST IN TIME' concept no longer being a catchy phrase; with growing demands for on- time deliveries; with the need to trim down expenses; and with point-to-point delivery requirements from suppliers and buyers alike, the company believes that Complete Logistics Solutions powered by Technology is the concept of today.

As such, the Global Group was established to cater to the requirements of the trade and to march into the future with a “Quest to Excel”.
